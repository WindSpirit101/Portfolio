Tropical Bay Breeze
  1. 2 ounces pineapple juice
  2. 2 ounces cranberry juice
  3. 1 ounce coconut malibu rum

Combine all ingredients. Pour over ice & garnish with oranges, pineapple or cherries

Dirty Monkey Drink
  1. banana
  2. 1.5 ounce Aged Rum
  3. 2 tablespoon Banana Liqueur
  4. 1/2 ounce Kahlua
  5. 1 dollop Coconut Cream
  6. 2 ounces Pineapple Juice
  7. Chocolate Syrup (for garnish)
  8. Whipped Cream (for garnish)

Blend banana, Kahula, banana liqueur, aged rum, ice, and coconut cream. Drizzle some chocolate syrup inside a tall glass, and pour the mixture. Garnish it with whipped cream, chocolate syrup
